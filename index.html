Experience

QUALCOMM | SOFTWARE ENGINEER INTERN | AUTOMOTIVE AUDIO TEAM

Sep 2019 - Aug 2020 | Markham, Canada

In 2019 and 2020, I did my professional experience year (PEY) at Qualcomm as a software engineer Intern of the automotive audio team, specializing in audio-related software on Qualcomm’s automotive platform. This internship has proved to be a precious and productive experience for me. I closely participated in the audio projects on various operating systems, including Blackberry QNX and different versions of Android Automotive (P, Q, and R). I designed and built an application that demonstrates the “multi-Zone” feature, allowing simultaneous music playback on different audio zones with Qualcomm’s hardware. I also enhanced a QNX audio application with the recording feature and propagated an audio post-processing application from Android P to Q, and R. These applications would be handy tools in internal testing and customer showcases. Furthermore, I collaborated with other engineers, helped bug fixing of audio drivers, and validated ported features on different hardware and operating systems.
